Privacy Policy for BibleJourney

Last Updated: April 9, 2026

Welcome to BibleJourney. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any information you choose to share with us. This policy explains our practices regarding data collection and usage.

Privacy Commitment: BibleJourney is primarily an offline, privacy-first application. We do not track your reading habits, and we do not sell your data to third parties.

1. Data Collection & Usage

BibleJourney does not automatically collect any personally identifiable information. However, we provide an optional feedback mechanism where you may choose to provide the following:

  • Name (Optional): Used to address you in our correspondence.
  • Email Address (Optional): Used solely to reply to your feedback, bug reports, or feature requests.
  • Message Content: The information you provide regarding your experience with the app.

2. Data Safety Table

In compliance with Google Play requirements, here is how we handle user data:

Data Type Collected Shared Purpose
Name / Email Yes (Optional) No App Support / Feedback
Personal Study Data (Notes, Bookmarks) No (Stored Locally) No Personalization

3. Local Storage

To provide a seamless experience, the following information is stored locally on your device (not on our servers):

  • Bookmarks and Verse Highlights
  • Personal Study Notes
  • Reading History and App Settings

This data remains on your device and is deleted if the application is uninstalled (unless backed up via your device's system backup).

4. Permissions

The app may request access to:

  • Internet: To download Bible versions and send feedback.
  • Storage: To save your study data on your device.

5. Third-Party Services

We do not use third-party tracking, analytics, or advertising SDKs. Your interaction with the Bible remains private and undisturbed.
