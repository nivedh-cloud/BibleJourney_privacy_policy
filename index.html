Privacy Policy for BibleJourney

Last Updated: April 9, 2026

Welcome to BibleJourney. Your privacy is critically important to us. This policy explains how our mobile application handles your data.

Summary: BibleJourney is a privacy-first application. We do not collect, store, or share your personal data on any external servers.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII). We do not require you to create an account, provide an email address, or share your contact details to use the app.

2. Local Storage

All your app activity, including bookmarks, highlights, personal notes, and reading history, is stored locally on your device's internal storage. We have no access to this information.

3. Permissions

The app may request the following permissions to function correctly:

  • Storage: To save your personal study data and preferences on your device.
  • Internet: To download Bible translations and display developer information.

4. Third-Party Services

BibleJourney does not share any data with third-party services, advertisers, or analytics providers. Your usage remains completely private.

5. Children's Privacy

Our app is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We encourage you to review this page periodically for any changes.
